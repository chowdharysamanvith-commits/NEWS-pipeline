1. Overview
The pharmaceutical landscape for bispecific antibodies and next-generation modalities demonstrates significant venture capital inflows alongside mixed regulatory outcomes in early 2026. Sidewinder Therapeutics secured substantial funding to advance its bispecific antibody-drug conjugate (ADC) platform, while Gilead Sciences acquired Ouro Medicines to integrate a bispecific T-cell engager into its pipeline. Regulatory milestones include FDA Orphan Drug Designation for Compass Therapeutics’ tovecimig and FDA approval for a teclistamab combination in multiple myeloma, contrasted by an ODAC vote against Roche’s glofitamab registration intent. Sanofi reported dual Phase 2 wins for lunsekimig in respiratory indications but missed the primary endpoint in atopic dermatitis. Strategic partnerships and licensing deals, such as Earendil Labs’ agreement with Sanofi and Boehringer Ingelheim’s deal with Simcere, highlight continued industry investment in autoimmune and oncology bispecifics. 2. Key Developments
• Sidewinder Therapeutics — SWT012 — Solid Tumors — Bispecific ADC, IND filing planned by end of 2026 (https://fiercebiotech.com/biotech/orbimeds-sidewinder-slithers-toward-clinic-137m-series-b-antibody-drug-conjugates).
• Gilead Sciences — OM336 (gamgertamig) — Autoimmune Conditions — Bispecific T-cell engager (BCMAxCD3), registrational trial planned for 2027 (https://fiercebiotech.com/biotech/3-new-acquisitions-digest-gilead-shifts-focus-pipeline-has-never-been-stronger).
• Sanofi — Lunsekimig — Asthma/Chronic Rhinosinusitis/Atopic Dermatitis — Phase 2b/2a (Positive in asthma and nasal polyps; negative in eczema) (https://fiercebiotech.com/biotech/sanofis-bispecific-scores-double-respiratory-phase-2-wins-flunks-eczema-study-0).
• Roche — Glofitamab-gxbm — Diffuse Large B-Cell Lymphoma — Phase 3 STARGLO (ODAC voted against registration intent) (https://cancernetwork.com/view/elucidating-bispecific-impact-in-diffuse-large-b-cell-lymphoma).
• AbbVie/Genmab — Epcoritamab-bysp — Diffuse Large B-Cell Lymphoma — Phase 3 EPCORE DLBCL-1 (Awaiting efficacy readout) (https://cancernetwork.com/view/elucidating-bispecific-impact-in-diffuse-large-b-cell-lymphoma).
• Compass Therapeutics / ABL Bio — Tovecimig (CTX-009) — Biliary Tract Cancer — FDA Orphan Drug Designation granted (https://cancernetwork.com/view/fda-grants-orphan-drug-designation-to-tovecimig-for-biliary-tract-cancer).
• Context Therapeutics — CTIM-76 — Platinum-Resistant Ovarian Cancer — Phase 1a/b (FDA Fast Track Designation granted) (https://cancernetwork.com/view/ctim-76-receives-fda-fast-track-status-in-platinum-resistant-ovarian-cancer).
• Teclistamab — BCMA Bispecific — Relapsed/Refractory Multiple Myeloma — FDA approved in combination with daratumumab (https://cancernetwork.com/view/teclistamab-combo-approval-is-excellent-for-r-r-multiple-myeloma).
• NovaBridge — Givastomig — Gastric Cancer — Phase 1b/3 (FDA confirmed accelerated approval pathway eligibility) (https://globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/04/07/3269679/0/en/NovaBridge-Reports-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results-and-Provides-Business-Update.html).
• AstraZeneca — Imfinzi/Imjudo — Unresectable Liver Cancer — EMERALD-3 Phase III trial (Significant progression-free survival benefits) (https://pharmabiz.com/NewsDetails.aspx?aid=185172&sid=2).

4. Clinical & Scientific Highlights
• Lunsekimig mechanism: Bispecific Nanobody constructed of five linked antibody fragments blocking thymic stromal lymphopoietin (TSLP) and interleukin-13 (IL-13) (https://fiercebiotech.com/biotech/sanofis-bispecific-scores-double-respiratory-phase-2-wins-flunks-eczema-study-0).
• Givastomig mechanism: Bispecific antibody targeting Claudin 18.2-positive tumor cells and conditionally activating T cells through the 4-1BB signaling pathway (https://globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/04/07/3269679/0/en/NovaBridge-Reports-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results-and-Provides-Business-Update.html).
• Givastomig Phase 1b results: 75% objective response rate (ORR) and 16.9-month median progression-free survival (mPFS) in first-line gastric cancer (https://globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/04/07/3269679/0/en/NovaBridge-Reports-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results-and-Provides-Business-Update.html).
• Tovecimig (CTX-009) mechanism: Targets DLL4 and VEGF-A signaling pathways to inhibit tumor angiogenesis (https://cancernetwork.com/view/fda-grants-orphan-drug-designation-to-tovecimig-for-biliary-tract-cancer).
• Tovecimig Trial Results: Overall Response Rate (ORR) 17.1% vs 5.3% (P = .031) compared to paclitaxel monotherapy (https://cancernetwork.com/view/fda-grants-orphan-drug-designation-to-tovecimig-for-biliary-tract-cancer).
• Teclistamab mechanism: BCMA bispecific monoclonal antibody (https://cancernetwork.com/view/teclistamab-combo-approval-is-excellent-for-r-r-multiple-myeloma).
• Teclistamab Trial Results: 83% of patients remained alive and progression-free at 3 years in MajesTEC-3 trial (https://cancernetwork.com/view/teclistamab-combo-approval-is-excellent-for-r-r-multiple-myeloma).
• CTIM-76 mechanism: CLDN6/CD3 bispecific antibody with monovalent CD3-binding domain and silenced Fc region (https://cancernetwork.com/view/ctim-76-receives-fda-fast-track-status-in-platinum-resistant-ovarian-cancer).
• Epcoritamab mechanism: DuoBody® CD3xCD20 bispecific antibody (https://cancernetwork.com/view/elucidating-bispecific-impact-in-diffuse-large-b-cell-lymphoma).
• Imfinzi/Imjudo Trial: EMERALD-3 phase III trial demonstrated statistically significant improvement in progression-free survival in unresectable liver cancer (https://pharmabiz.com/NewsDetails.aspx?aid=185172&sid=2).
• Amivantamab mechanism: Subcutaneous formulation evaluated for EGFR-mutated Advanced NSCLC (https://cancernetwork.com/view/subcutaneous-amivantamab-is-a-way-to-move-forward-in-egfr-nsclc-care).
• Amivantamab Trial Results: PALOMA-2 trial data showed an 85% response rate and median progression-free survival above 26 months (https://cancernetwork.com/view/subcutaneous-amivantamab-is-a-way-to-move-forward-in-egfr-nsclc-care).
