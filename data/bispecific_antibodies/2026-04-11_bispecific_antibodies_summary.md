1. Overview
The pharmaceutical landscape for bispecific antibodies and related biologics demonstrates significant activity across acquisitions, clinical trials, and regulatory approvals during the reporting period. Sidewinder Therapeutics secured substantial funding to advance next-generation bispecific antibody-drug conjugates (ADCs) into clinical testing (https://biopharmadive.com/news/sidewinder-bispecific-antibody-drug-conjugates-orbimed-financing/816942/). Gilead Sciences expanded its pipeline through the acquisition of Ouro Medicines, securing a bispecific T-cell engager for autoimmune indications (https://fiercebiotech.com/biotech/3-new-acquisitions-digest-gilead-shifts-focus-pipeline-has-never-been-stronger). Sanofi reported mixed clinical outcomes for its bispecific Nanobody lunsekimig, achieving respiratory disease wins but failing an eczema study (https://fiercebiotech.com/biotech/sanofis-bispecific-scores-double-respiratory-phase-2-wins-flunks-eczema-study-0). Concurrently, the FDA approved teclistamab-cqyv plus daratumumab for relapsed/refractory multiple myeloma, establishing a new standard of care (https://cancernetwork.com/view/teclistamab-combo-approval-is-excellent-for-r-r-multiple-myeloma). Market analysis projects the global bispecific antibodies market to reach $22.3 billion by 2030 driven by immuno-oncology innovation (https://globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/04/10/3271710/0/en/Global-Bispecific-Antibodies-Market-to-Reach-22-3-Billion-by-2030-as-Immuno-Oncology-Innovation-and-R-D-Investment-Accelerate.html).

4. Clinical & Scientific Highlights
• Tovecimig (CTX-009) — Mechanism of Action: Targets DLL4 and VEGF-A signaling pathways to inhibit tumor angiogenesis and vascularization (https://cancernetwork.com/view/fda-grants-orphan-drug-designation-to-tovecimig-for-biliary-tract-cancer)
• Tovecimig (CTX-009) — Trial Phase: Phase 2/3 COMPANION-002 (https://cancernetwork.com/view/fda-grants-orphan-drug-designation-to-tovecimig-for-biliary-tract-cancer)
• Tovecimig (CTX-009) — Patient Population: Unresectable advanced, metastatic, or recurrent biliary tract cancer patients who progressed following first-line gemcitabine and platinum-based chemotherapy (https://cancernetwork.com/view/fda-grants-orphan-drug-designation-to-tovecimig-for-biliary-tract-cancer)
• Tovecimig (CTX-009) — Clinical Endpoints: Primary endpoint of overall response rate was 17.1% in the combination arm versus 5.3% in the control arm (P = .031) (https://cancernetwork.com/view/fda-grants-orphan-drug-designation-to-tovecimig-for-biliary-tract-cancer)
• Lunsekimig — Mechanism blocks thymic stromal lymphopoietin (TSLP) and interleukin-13 (IL-13) — Indication Moderate-to-severe asthma and chronic rhinosinusitis — Phase 2b Aircules and Phase 2a Duet — Result statistically significant reduction in exacerbations and nasal polyps (https://fiercebiotech.com/biotech/sanofis-bispecific-scores-double-respiratory-phase-2-wins-flunks-eczema-study-0)
• Lunsekimig — Mechanism blocks TSLP and IL-13 — Indication Moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis — Phase 2b Velvet — Result missed primary mission of demonstrating reduction in eczema severity (https://fiercebiotech.com/biotech/sanofis-bispecific-scores-double-respiratory-phase-2-wins-flunks-eczema-study-0)
• Givastomig — Mechanism targets Claudin 18.2-positive tumor cells and conditionally activates T cells through 4-1BB signaling — Indication First line Her2-, CLDN 18.2+, PD-L1+ gastroesophageal cancer — Phase 1b — Result 75% objective response rate and 16.9-month median progression free survival (https://globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/04/07/3269679/0/en/NovaBridge-Reports-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results-and-Provides-Business-Update.html)
• Teclistamab-cqyv + Daratumumab — Mechanism: Teclistamab is a BCMA bispecific monoclonal antibody; Daratumumab is an anti-CD38 monoclonal antibody (https://cancernetwork.com/view/teclistamab-combo-approval-is-excellent-for-r-r-multiple-myeloma)
• Teclistamab-cqyv + Daratumumab — Trial Phase: MajesTEC-3 (Phase 3) (https://cancernetwork.com/view/teclistamab-combo-approval-is-excellent-for-r-r-multiple-myeloma)
• Teclistamab-cqyv + Daratumumab — Clinical Endpoints: Primary endpoint PFS showed a hazard ratio of 0.17 with 83% of patients alive and progression-free at 3 years in the teclistamab/daratumumab arm (https://cancernetwork.com/view/teclistamab-combo-approval-is-excellent-for-r-r-multiple-myeloma)
• OM336 (gamgertamig) — Mechanism binds BCMA and CD3 on surface of B cells — Indication B and plasma cell-driven autoimmune diseases — Phase Registrational trial planned for next year (https://fiercebiotech.com/biotech/3-new-acquisitions-digest-gilead-shifts-focus-pipeline-has-never-been-stronger)
• Bispecific ADCs (Sidewinder) — Mechanism: Able to recognize and bind to protein combinations that are highly expressed on cancer cells, consisting of one protein receptor that helps drive cancer and another that pulls substances into cells (https://biopharmadive.com/news/sidewinder-bispecific-antibody-drug-conjugates-orbimed-financing/816942/)
• Bispecific ADCs (Sidewinder) — Trial Phase: Pre-clinical/Early Development with expectation to push lead program into clinical development in 2027 (https://biopharmadive.com/news/sidewinder-bispecific-antibody-drug-conjugates-orbimed-financing/816942/)
